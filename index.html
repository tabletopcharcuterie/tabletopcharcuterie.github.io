    Who we are

    Hi! I'm Annie. I'm the owner and creator of Tabletop Charcuterie. I first fell in love with charcuterie during my honeymoon trip to Italy. We found this quaint little charcuterie bar in a less visited area of Rome and decided to go inside. The variety and choices that takes place in assembling and eating the board fascinated me. Coming home to Vancouver, I started looking for local sources of charcuterie meats and cheeses to create my own.

    I started making charcuterie displays for many of my social gatherings. My friends then started to ask me to make them for their bridal showers, girls night in, birthday parties, and so on. They always seem to be a crowd favorite, and definitely not camera shy.

    With the pandemic happening in 2020, I don't get to create these boards as much as I like to. Thus, I decided to start my own business of making charcuterie boxes and boards to share my love of charcuterie with others.

    Each of our board is a unique smorgasbord of flavour combinations and ideas that I am discovering and learning about each day. Our boards and boxes are affordably priced, so we can share our creations with as many people possible. Come join me in my journey discovering the world of charcuterie!

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How much is it for delivery?

    Delivery is free for Surrey, Langley, Delta, Richmond, and New Westminster. Other areas of the Lower Mainland can have free delivery with orders over $150. Otherwise, we will let you know the delivery costs (~$20-$30 depending on distance from Surrey)

    I want to pick up my own box, where are you located?

    We are located in Surrey Sullivan Heights area. We will let you know the pick up location once the order is confirmed

    How do I confirm an order?

    Once we have responded to your order request, we will send you an email for you to E-Transfer the payment. At least 50% must be received 48 hours before your order is to be delivered.

    Can I request specific items or modify the box?

    Items in the box, especially fruits and vegetables are quite seasonal, but we will try our best to accommodate. If you have any dietary needs (or got a picky eater around) please let us know.
